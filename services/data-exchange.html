Data Integrations with CustomFit Footwear Program

Along with providing top quality safety footwear, Lehigh believes excellent service and maintaining efficiency and security are essential to doing good business. Our Employee Data Exchange option will help you better manage your Corporate Safety Shoe Program.

What Does The Data Integration Involve?

Your CustomFit safety footwear website will be loaded with your specific employee data which will allow for greater management of their eligibility and your entire safety footwear program. This integration of employee data into our system will provide you with many benefits, especially if you have a large number of employees to manage and buy from us exclusively.

Employee Data Integration Features and Benefits

  • Employees can place their own orders
  • Set employee eligibility to automatically reset based on start date or last purchase date
  • Customized checkout page that includes and pre-fills with whatever necessary information you require and request including a Purchase Order Number, Employee ID, Department Name, etc.
  • Customized safety shoe style selection shown by department or location
