As you know, your feet are 3-dimensional - length, width and height. Unfortunately, all tools used to measure feet are only 2-dimensional - length and width. Footwear Sizing Tips

  • If you are a woman planning to buy a men's style simply subtract 1.5 sizes from your current size. For example: If you wear a women's size 9, you would wear a men's size 7.5.
  • If your foot length or width numbers are between sizes, order a half size up.
  • Your left foot and right foot may not be the exact same size, so measure both and go with the largest.
  • From morning to evening your feet will swell. Best time to measure is afternoon or later after you have been on your feet for a an extended period of time.
  • If you have any doubts about your true foot size you can visit a local shoe retailer to have your foot measured with a brannock devise.
