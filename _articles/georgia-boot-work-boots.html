Composite Toe

For lightweight protection that passes easily through metal detectors Georgia Boot offers composite toe styles.

Composite toe boots are popular and some of our best selling and top reviewed styles because of their excellent protection for airport and security workers. Georgia Boot offers them in both men's and women's options.

ANTImpact Metatarsal-Guard Boots

For even greater protection of the foot, Georgia Boot also offers ANTImpact™ Metatarsal Guard boots.

If extra protection to the metatarsal bones in the top of the foot is an important consideration for your work environment, be sure to check out all of the Georgia Boots Met Guard styles including those with their exclusive ANTImpact™ material that is flexible but firms instantly when struck to offer the same level of protection as rigid met guards.

Static Dissipative

Georgia Boot also offers a men's and women's selection of static dissipative shoes and boots for jobs that work with sensitive electronic parts and equipment.

Check out our article on Static Dissipative Shoes to further help you understand when you should wear this protection.

Comfortable Work Boots

Georgia Boot was the first brand to deliver true comfort in work boots with the innovation of Comfort Core® Technology and continues to develop superior comfort features.

Their patented Comfort Core® insole design incorporates an energy-return heel plug that stores energy as it's compressed by the wearer's foot and then releases it as the foot is lifted. This revolutionary feature has lead to other insole technology such as the AMP™ (Advanced Memory Polyurethane) footbed which has a unique dual-layer of Polyurethane and Memory Foam and the CC5 footbed with ergonomic arch support and superior airflow for cooling.

Outsole Material Strength

For farm and ranch work, Georgia Boot styles exceed with specialized features such as SPR™ leather and the tough Carbo-Tec™ outsole.

Because of a special tanning process, SPR™ leather is 3 times stronger and 2.5 times more abrasion resistant than conventional leather offering protection against barnyard and pasture chemicals. Built with durable, long-lasting real rubber combined with a unique blend of proprietary materials, the Carbo-Tec™ outsole is chemical, oil and highly slip-resistant.
