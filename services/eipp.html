EIPP Electronic Invoice Presentment & Payment

We make your life easier and help conserve our natural resources with electronic invoices, receipts, and payment. As a Lehigh customer, you'll automatically have it all continuously at your fingertips through Electronic Invoice Presentment & Payment (EIPP).

  • No more lost invoices
  • Review invoices online
  • No more waiting for the mail
  • Account access 24/7/365
  • Invoice notices emailed daily
  • Can be sent to more than one person in your company
  • Pay online by credit card or echeck
  • Receive payment verification
  • Less keying by your employees

Review Online Invoices Receipts

  • Employee Name, ID Number and Department
  • Product style, size and price
  • Subsidy (if applicable)
  • Cash paid
  • Balance due (for payroll deduction)
  • Sales receipts with employee signature
  • See all totals

See Your Account Statement Online

  • Amount due
  • Due dates
  • Invoice numbers
  • Purchase Order numbers

You'll be able to view all new and previous invoices and receipts and have the freedom to print or export any date range of statements into Excel. You can customize, sort and organize for fast and effecient record-keeping.

All purchases can be paid online too! We accept Mastercard, Visa, American Express and Discover.

Save Time. Reduce Waste. 24/7 Access.

How to Access Your Account Statements

After your first Lehigh CustomFit purchase you will automatically receive an email notification with an invoice PDF file. You can then review this invoice and other account information online by logging into your EIPP account.
