How To Login

  1. Enter your 6-digit Customer Number, Location Number & Password in the spaces provided.
  2. Click the "Login" button. You will be redirected to the CustomFit website where you can begin your order.

How To Order For One Employee

  1. Select desired style.
  2. Using the drop downs, enter the quantity, select the width and size.
  3. Click "Add To Cart"
  4. Review your order and click "Checkout"
  5. Fill out the required shipping and billing information, including the Voucher
    6. Number.
  6. Review your info. and click "Pay Balance"

Ordering For Multiple Employees

  1. Click on the "Multi-Employee Ordering" link at the left of the home page.
  2. Using the drop downs, Select the style #, width, size, color and quantity.
  3. Enter the employee's information inculiding the P.O. and Voucher Number
  4. Click "Add To Cart";
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 to add products for other employees.
  6. Review your order and click "Submit"
  7. On the Check Out Page, fill out the required shipping and billing
    8. information, including the Voucher Number
  8. Review your info and click "Submit Order".

You will receive an email that will break the order down for you. You are done! Remember that you have FREE SHIPPING AND RETURNS. Your shoes will arrive in 5-7 days via Fedex ground.
